Methodology

Audit‑ready workflow

1 · Discovery & Scoping

Clarify policy/legal questions, scope sources and jurisdictions, and define success metrics.

2 · Corpus & Guardrails

Curate the source set; configure quality thresholds, disallowed sources, and bias checks.

3 · Grounded Generation

Produce claims with inline citations; every statement is traceable to original context.

4 · Human Review

Domain experts verify, challenge, and refine outputs for clarity and defensibility.

5 · Delivery & Handover

Provide briefs, explainations, and exports; set up monitoring for updates and regressions.

6 · Ongoing Support

Versioning, change logs, and quick‑turn updates as policy and case law evolve.
